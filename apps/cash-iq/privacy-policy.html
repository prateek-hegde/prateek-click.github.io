Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Cash IQ ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Access

  • SMS messages (financial messages only)
  • Local device storage (for backups and exports)

The App reads SMS messages solely to identify financial transactions such as bank debit, credit, salary, cashback, refund, EMI, and other financial notifications.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Detect financial transactions
  • Categorize expenses and income
  • Generate dashboards and reports
  • Learn your categorization preferences locally
  • Improve automatic transaction detection on your device

Local Processing

All SMS parsing, transaction extraction, rule matching, analytics, and learning occur locally on your device. Your financial information is not uploaded to our servers.

Artificial Intelligence

The App works offline. Future AI features, if introduced, will be optional and disabled by default unless you explicitly enable them. Any feature that transmits data over the internet will clearly request your consent.

Rule Updates

Future versions may download updated rule packs to improve transaction recognition. Rule packs contain application logic only and do not include or upload your financial data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal financial information with third parties.

Backups and Export

If you export your data, backup files are created locally on your device. You control how exported files are stored or shared.

Permissions

  • SMS: Required to detect financial transactions.
  • Storage: Required for importing and exporting backups.

Security

Your information is stored locally on your device. Protect your device with a screen lock and encryption where available.

Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the application evolves. The effective date will be updated whenever material changes are made.

Contact

Please use the contact information provided on the Google Play listing if you have questions about this Privacy Policy.

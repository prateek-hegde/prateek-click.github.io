Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2026

At Cash IQ, your privacy is our top priority. We designed Cash IQ as an "offline-first, privacy-first" expense tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

1. Data Storage and Processing (Offline-First)

Cash IQ is designed to operate primarily offline. All of your core financial data—including parsed transactions, merchants, categories, and settings—is stored locally on your device in an embedded database.

We do not maintain backend servers, nor do we sync your financial data to the cloud. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or back it up yourself.

2. SMS Permission and Usage

To automatically detect and categorize your expenses, Cash IQ requests the READ_SMS permission.

  • Local Processing Only: The SMS messages are read and processed entirely on your device. We use local rule engines and pattern matching to extract transaction details.
  • No Data Exfiltration: We do not upload, share, or sell your SMS messages or financial data to any third party. The contents of your inbox remain strictly on your device.

3. Crash Reporting and Analytics

To improve the stability and performance of Cash IQ, we use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports.

  • What we collect: Non-personally identifiable information such as device model, operating system version, and stack traces related to app crashes.
  • What we do NOT collect: We never attach your financial data, SMS contents, or PII to crash reports.
  • Opt-out: You can disable crash reporting at any time via the settings menu within the app. Furthermore, crash reporting is strictly disabled in debug/development environments.

4. Third-Party Services

While the app operates mostly offline, it integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Crashlytics: For anonymous crash reporting (as detailed above).

These services have their own privacy policies, which govern their use of any transmitted data.

5. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally, the security of your information relies heavily on the security of your device. We strongly recommend setting a device passcode, using biometrics, and utilizing Cash IQ's built-in App Lock feature (Local Auth) to prevent unauthorized access to your expense records.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, through an in-app notification.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the links provided in the app's "About" section.
